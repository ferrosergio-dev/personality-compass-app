1. General Information

The "Personality Compass" app is designed for personal growth, self-reflection, and inspiration through psychological and intuitive models: MindPath 16, MindPath 5D, Enneagram, journaling, and other tools.

❗ Important: This app is NOT a medical, psychological, or diagnostic tool. Test results are for informational and inspirational purposes only. The app does not replace professional consultation with a psychologist, psychotherapist, or physician. The author is not liable for any decisions made based on the app's output.

2. Data Collection and Use

The app does NOT collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data.

All data (test results, journal entries, settings) is stored only on your device and remains fully under your control. The app works offline — no internet is required for core functionality.

3. Internet Usage

Internet is used only if you choose to:

  • "Report an issue" – opens your email app
  • "Support the developer" – opens your banking app to scan a QR code or copy IBAN

In these cases, the developer does not receive your data — your device communicates directly with the email or bank service.

4. Third-party Services

The app does NOT use:

  • Advertising (e.g., Google AdMob)
  • Analytics (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase)
  • Cloud data storage
  • Social media integrations

5. GDPR and Your Rights

Since no personal data is collected, GDPR provisions regarding access, deletion, or data portability do not apply. All data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app.

6. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated. The latest version will always be available via the link in the app store description.

Thank you for using Personality Compass on your journey of self-discovery!
