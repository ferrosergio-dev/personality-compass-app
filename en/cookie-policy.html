❗ Important: This Cookie Policy applies to the website personality-compass.app, not to the mobile app. The mobile app works 100% offline and does not use cookies.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember your actions and preferences over time.

2. Cookies We Use

We use only analytical cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website. We do not use advertising cookies.

2.1 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect information about how visitors use our website. Google Analytics stores information about:

  • Pages you visit
  • Time spent on pages
  • How you arrived at our website
  • What device and browser you use

Google Analytics cookies:

  • _ga - expires after 2 years, used to distinguish users
  • _gid - expires after 24 hours, used to distinguish users
  • _gat - expires after 1 minute, used to throttle request rate

Learn more about Google's use of cookies.

2.2 Yandex Metrica

We use Yandex Metrica to analyze traffic and user behavior. Yandex Metrica stores information about:

  • Pages you visit
  • Mouse movements and clicks (Webvisor feature)
  • Device and browser information
  • Approximate geographic location (city/country)

Yandex Metrica cookies:

  • _ym_uid - expires after 1 year, user identifier
  • _ym_d - expires after 1 year, date of first visit
  • _ym_isad - expires after 1 day, ad blocker detection
  • _ym_visorc - session cookie, Webvisor settings

Learn more about Yandex's cookie policy.

2.3 Cloudflare Web Analytics

We also use Cloudflare Web Analytics, which does not use cookies and respects visitor privacy. It provides basic traffic statistics without tracking individuals.

3. How We Use Cookie Data

We use this information to:

  • Understand how many people visit our website
  • See which pages are most popular
  • Improve website content and user experience
  • Detect and fix technical issues

We do not:

  • Sell any data to third parties
  • Use cookies for advertising
  • Track users across different websites
  • Create personal profiles of visitors

4. Your Consent

When you first visit our website, we ask for your consent to use analytical cookies. You can choose to:

  • Accept – we will load Google Analytics and Yandex Metrica
  • Reject – only Cloudflare Web Analytics (cookie-free) will work

You can change your mind at any time by clearing your cookies and reloading the website.

5. Managing Cookies in Your Browser

Most web browsers allow you to manage cookies through their settings. You can:

  • Block all cookies
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Set preferences for specific websites

To learn how to manage cookies in your browser:

  • Chrome
  • Firefox
  • Safari
  • Edge

6. More Information

For more information about how we handle your data, please see our:

  • Privacy Policy (for the mobile app)
